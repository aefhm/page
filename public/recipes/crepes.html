Crêpes

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 cup water
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • pinch of salt

Steps

  1. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together flour and eggs.

  2. Gradually stir in milk and water, mixing to combine.

  3. Add melted butter and salt; beat until smooth.

  4. Heat a lightly oiled crêpe pan over medium heat.

  5. Pour approximately 1/4 cup batter into the pan, tilting to spread evenly.

  6. Cook for about 2 minutes until light brown, then flip and cook the other side.
