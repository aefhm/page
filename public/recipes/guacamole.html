Guacamole

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe avocados
  • 1 roma tomato, seeds removed
  • 1/4 red onion
  • 1 serrano pepper
  • 2 limes
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • kosher salt
  • olive oil, optional

Steps

  1. Cube the avocados, then mash them lightly, leaving some texture.

  2. Remove the tomato seeds, then chop the tomato, red onion, cilantro, and serrano.

  3. Mix everything together.

  4. Juice the limes into the mixture.

  5. Add kosher salt to taste.

Notes

Olive oil is optional; drizzle if the avocados are dry.

Add a small pinch of cumin.
