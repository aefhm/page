Guacamole

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe avocados
  • 1 roma tomato
  • 1 red onion
  • 1 jalapeño
  • 2 limes
  • cilantro

Steps

  1. Cube the avocados.

  2. Chop the onion, tomato, cilantro, and jalapeño.

  3. Mix everything together.

  4. Juice the limes into the mixture.

  5. Add salt to taste.
