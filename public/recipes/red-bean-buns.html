Red Bean Buns

Ingredients

  • sweet potatoes
  • glutinous rice powder
  • red bean paste

Steps

  1. Peel sweet potatoes.

  2. Soften the sweet potatoes by steaming or microwaving, then make them into a paste.

  3. Add glutinous rice powder to the paste and mix.

  4. Add sweet red bean paste filling and shape into a bun.

  5. Coat both sides of the bun with a dash of oil.

  6. Preheat oven to 350-400 F.

  7. Bake for 15 minutes, flipping halfway through.
