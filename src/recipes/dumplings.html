Dumplings

Ingredients

  • Shrimp
  • Cilantro
  • Soy sauce
  • 3 cups flour
  • 1 cup water

Steps

  1. Gradually mix water into the flour to make the dough.
  2. Roll dough into a churro.
  3. Cut dough into Reese's pieces sizes and flatten.
  4. Fill the dumpling skin.
  5. Boil water, and cook for ~5 minutes.
