V60 Pour Over Coffee

Ingredients

  • 15g light roast coffee (medium grind)
  • 250ml water at 95°C (203°F)
  • V60 dripper and filter

Steps

  1. Set kettle to 95°C (203°F). Rinse V60 filter and place 15g ground coffee in dripper.
  2. Bloom (0:00-0:45): Pour 30ml water over grounds, swirl gently, wait 45 seconds.
  3. Main pour (0:45-1:45): Pour remaining 220ml water in slow spiral, staying in center.
  4. Finish: Give gentle swirl when bed drops, let drain completely (~3 minutes total).
