V60 Pour Over Coffee

A compact V60 routine based on the Hoffman method.

International · Makes 1 · 4 minutes

Ingredients

  • 15g light roast coffee (medium grind)
  • 250ml water at 95°C (203°F)
  • V60 dripper and filter

Steps

  1. Set kettle to 95°C (203°F). Rinse the V60 filter and place 15g ground coffee in the dripper.

  2. Bloom (0:00-0:45): pour 30ml water over the grounds, swirl gently, and wait 45 seconds.

  3. Main pour (0:45-1:45): pour the remaining 220ml water in a slow spiral, staying in the center.

  4. Finish with a gentle swirl when the bed drops, then let the brewer drain completely.
